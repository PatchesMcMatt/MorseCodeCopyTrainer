How to Use This Trainer

Word List

- Load a plain text file containing one or more words per line.

Speed Settings

- Character WPM controls dot/dash speed.
- Farnsworth WPM controls spacing speed.
- Effective WPM shows true overall speed.

Side Tone

- Choose the audio frequency used for Morse playback.

Practice

- Click Start to begin practice session. Head copy the word sent and type it into the text field. Press Enter to submit. If you need to hear the word again, press Enter before typing any part of the word.
- A performance summary will be displayed once all words in the imported word list have been copied.

Performance Tracking

- Your average response time for the last 25 correct words is displayed.
- The average number of times you need to hear a word before you submit your copy is displayed as "Average Plays".

History

- Incorrect characters appear in red.
